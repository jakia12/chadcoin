Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $CHAD. If you dont have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
